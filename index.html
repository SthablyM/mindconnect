The inspiration behind my project

The inspiration behind this project comes from a deeply personal observation. Over the years, I have witnessed how many young people struggle with their mental health but lack access to proper care and resources. It became evident to me that mental health facilities are often inaccessible due to high costs, which leaves these individuals with few options. Iâ€™ve seen firsthand the toll this can take, leading to a growing crisis where the number of suicide cases continues to rise. This project is my way of addressing that gap and creating a platform that not only raises awareness but also provides support to those who need it most. While this is my Portfolio Project for Holberton School, it holds much more significance to me personally. It represents my commitment to making a difference in mental health by using technology as a bridge to connect those who are struggling with the help they need.
